Who are we?

Photo World Lab is a wholesaler and retailer of a wide range of Photo print sizes, P Large format printing , gifts items, Uniforms (Schools, Security companies, Corporate entities) and Embroidery products supplying both the South African and international markets. We produce beautiful products created by our creative staff. We are also a supplier of handmade paper packages. We have done many wedding functions with invitation cards, Wedding programmes, Wedding photobooks and albums. We print all sizes of photos and frame sizes. We do and have done school packages for many schools both primary and creches. We do School certificates, Calendars, Key holders, branded Mugs, Fridge magnets, Plates, snow balls, Certificate folders, Class photos, Group photos, PVC school Identification cards (Plastic). We do embroidery for many schools, security companies, SAMWU, CEPPWAWU, DoJ, SADTU, World Kidney Day, Capricorn district National school Nutrition programme-Aprons, Tvet College Kind Sabata Dalindyebo District Eastern Cape., Embroidery for the department of Health-Gauteng, KZN, Limpopo and North West. We do Slick Screening and making of sports Uniforms, Security Uniforms, Coporate uniforms, bags and Church Flags. Our Vision To form partnerships with communities of African designers to create beautiful, innovative handmade products by marrying contemporary design and traditional taste and style in an environment that supports a sustainable income for our staff and their families.
